Family Safety Guide

Before Your Hike

  • Review trail details and safety ratings
  • Check weather forecast and trail conditions
  • Preview AI narratives and set content controls
  • Pack essentials: water, snacks, first aid kit
  • Share your hiking plan with a trusted contact

During Your Adventure

  • Stay on marked trails at all times
  • Keep the family group together
  • Use the check-in feature regularly
  • Monitor weather alerts within the app
  • Take breaks when needed - especially for younger children

Emergency Procedures

  • Use the SOS button for immediate assistance
  • Follow the offline emergency guide if without service
  • Activate the location beacon to help rescuers find you
  • Utilize the built-in first aid instructions
